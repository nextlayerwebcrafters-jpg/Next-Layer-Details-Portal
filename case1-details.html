Overview

We built a professional, high-performance website for a local business that had no online presence. The site features clean design, fast loading times, and clear CTAs to drive customer inquiries and conversions.

Problem & Challenge

Problem: Local business had no online presence, missing out on potential customers searching for their services.

Impact: Lost leads, no way for customers to find contact info or service details, weak competitive positioning.

Our Approach

  • Built a responsive, fast-loading static website using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap
  • Designed clear service pages with engaging copy and compelling CTAs
  • Implemented contact forms and direct inquiry channels
  • Optimized for mobile and desktop viewing
  • Set up basic SEO fundamentals (meta tags, structure, performance)
  • Deployed on reliable, fast hosting for optimal performance

Results Achieved

  • ✓ 50+ monthly inquiries from website
  • ✓ Improved local search visibility
  • ✓ Fast page load times (<2 seconds)
  • ✓ Professional brand presence established
